Welcome to Project Green.

Project Green is an Android app focused on recycling and saving energy. Open the app from anywhere in the world, and find recycling bins that other users have marked. Also, find information and advice on how to save money and energy with automobiles, laundry, lights, etc.

Authors and Contributors

In December 2015, high school junior Nolan Blankenau (@Elden123) and sophomore Stephanie Fu (@dash102) began developing Project Green with one goal: to allow users to come together to help the planet. Five months later, the first version of Project Green was completed. Additions and other enhancements are currently being developed.
